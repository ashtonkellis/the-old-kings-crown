# The Old King's Crown — Rules Reference

> "A Tale of Royalty, Rebels and Ruses."
> © Eerie Idol Games 2025 — Game Design by Pablo Clark

---

## Overview

2–4 player asymmetric strategy game. Players are heirs competing to claim the old king's vacant throne by accruing the most **Influence (coins)** over a series of Rounds. Each Round represents one year and is divided into five Phases.

**Win condition:** Most Influence in your Supply at the end of the final Round.

---

## Game Length

| Mode     | Rounds |
|----------|--------|
| Short    | 4      |
| Standard | 5 *(recommended)* |
| Extended | 6      |

## The Four Factions

| Faction       | Playstyle                                      | Heir                  |
|---------------|------------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| The Nobility  | Potent defence, sustain, control               | Titus of Dawns & Dusks |
| The Clans     | Mobility, momentum, overwhelming force         | Saga the Unifier      |
| The Uprising  | Scheming, feints, eliminating rival cards      | The Secret Society    |
| The Gathering | Card manipulation, subversion, creeping power  | Petrichor the Unmasked|

Each Faction has identical Basic Faction Cards but unique:
- **Advanced Faction Cards** (acquired with Lore, added to Deck)
- **HQ Cards** (acquired with Lore, go to Supply)
- **4 Tactic Tiles** (powerful one-shot or multi-use abilities)
- **Kingdom's Favour Action** (Faction-specific, 3 uses)

## The Kingdom Board

### The Map — Three Regions, Six Locations

| Region | Locations | Location Rewards |
|---|---|---|
| **The Highlands** | Castle, Wilderness | Castle: Govern with 1 card (discard all others in that Council); Wilderness: Journey with 1 Active/Hand card |
| **The Plateau** | Harvest Field, Battlefield | Harvest Field: Gain 1 Influence + claim Kingdom's Favour; Battlefield: Gain 2 Influence |
| **The Lowlands** | Shrine, Necropolis | Shrine: Move up to 3 cards Active/Hand to bottom of Deck; Necropolis: Shuffle Discard Pile + draw up to 3 cards, may discard any number from Hand |

### Other Zones

- **Order Track** — shows turn order; higher Influence = higher position; first player holds Active Player Marker
- **Great Road** — 4 face-up Kingdom Cards (market); leftmost space holds face-down Kingdom Deck
- **Round Track** — 6 slots; marker advances each Winter
- **Councils Tile** — three separate Councils (see Councils section)
- **Kingdom Discard** — public, face-up; discarded Kingdom Cards go here
- **Lost Pile** — communal; Eliminated cards, spent Supporters, and Journeyed cards go here; public
- **Reserve** — communal bank of Influence and Lore tokens

---

## Player Board

Each player's play area contains:
- **Supply** — space around Player Board; holds Influence, Lore, acquired HQ Cards, certain Kingdom Cards
- **Hand** — private (discussable, not showable)
- **Deck** — face-down Faction Cards (left of board)
- **Discard Pile** — face-up Faction Cards (right of board)
- **Herald Slot** — holds Herald piece
- **Supporter Slots** — holds 5 Supporter pieces
- **Kingdom Card Spaces** — 2 slots (each Occupied by a Faction Card slotted beneath)
- **Tactic Slots** — 4 Tactic Tiles
- **Hand Size Marker** — tracks current Hand Size
- **Site of Power** — separate from Supply; holds Advanced Faction Cards and HQ Cards
- **Kingdom's Favour slot** — holds the disc when claimed

### Starting Hand Size

6 cards (includes Heir card + 5 drawn from shuffled Deck).

## Faction Cards

### Basic Faction Cards
- Identical across Factions (cosmetic differences only)
- Start in Deck/Hand

### Advanced Faction Cards
- Unique per Faction
- Start on Site of Power; acquired with Lore
- When acquired → go to Hand (if Hand is full, go to top of Deck)

### Card Anatomy
- **Strength** (top-left) — used in Bids and Clashes
- **Archetype** — Agent, Captain, Cavalry, Champion, Follower, Heir, Ruse, Trader, War Machine (cosmetic, may be targeted by abilities)
- **Traits** (upper-left corner) — passive, always active
- **Commands** (printed on card) — activated in specific Steps
- **Votes** (hammer icon) — contributed when card is placed in a Council
- **Lore** (scroll icon) — Lore tokens gained when card is Journeyed
- **♦ icon** — card starts in your Hand
- **Rules Text** — Actions, Effects, or abilities

### HQ Cards
- No Strength, Archetype, or Traits
- Start on Site of Power; acquired with Lore
- When acquired → go to Supply (persistent passive abilities)

## Sequence of Play

### Phase 1: Start of the Year *(skip Round 1)*

**Step 1 — Draw Cards** *(simultaneously)*
- Draw from Deck up to your full Hand Size.
- **Attrition:** If Deck is empty when you must draw → shuffle Discard Pile into new Deck, reduce Hand Size by 1 (min 3), then draw. Hand Size min 3, max 8.

**Step 2 — Determine Turn Order** *(simultaneously)*
- Players adjust Order Track based on Influence totals. Most Influence = first position.
- Tie: reverse the previous order of tied players.

---

### Phase 2: Spring

**Step 1 — Place Bids** *(simultaneously)*
- Each player selects one Faction Card from Hand and places it face-down in front of them. This is their Bidding card.

**Step 2 — Resolve Bids** *(in Bidding order: highest Strength first; ties broken by Order Track position)*
- Reveal all Bidding cards simultaneously.
- Each player's Bidding Strength = card's Strength + any applicable ability bonuses.
- Resolve from highest to lowest Bidding Strength. Choose **one**:
  - **I. Take a Kingdom Card from the Great Road** and Occupy it with Bidding card.
  - **II. Steal an Occupied Kingdom Card** from an opponent (must have Bidding Strength **higher** than the Occupying card's Strength). Opponent's card returns to their Hand.
  - **III. Return Bidding card to Hand** (no Kingdom Card acquired).

**Occupying a Kingdom Card:**
- Place Kingdom Card on your Player Board (either empty space or replace existing one).
- Slot Bidding card beneath it — it Occupies the Kingdom Card.
- If replacing an existing Kingdom Card: discard it to Kingdom Discard; return its former Occupying card to your Hand.
- Bold **SETUP** text on a Kingdom Card must be resolved immediately upon acquisition.

**Repopulating the Great Road:**
1. If one or more cards were taken: move rightmost remaining card to Kingdom Discard; slide remaining cards right to fill spaces.
   - If no cards were taken: move the two rightmost cards to Kingdom Discard instead.
2. Draw and reveal new Kingdom Cards to fill all spaces (rightmost first). Finally flip the top of the Kingdom Deck face-up.

**Step 3 — Place Heralds and Cards**

*A. Place Heralds (in turn order):*
- Each player places their Herald piece on any Location on the Map (can share Locations).

*B. Place Cards Next to Regions (simultaneously):*
- Each player places **one** face-down Faction Card from their Hand next to **each of the three Regions** (3 cards total).
- Players may look at their own face-down cards at any time but not opponents' cards.

**Step 4 — Spring Action Step** *(in turn order)*
- Activate any number of Spring (leaf icon) Actions and Commands available to you.
- **Placing Supporters** is a Spring Action: move any number of Supporter pieces from your Player Board to any Regions.
- Supporters placed on Regions add **+1 Strength** to your first Clash in that Region this Round.

---

### Phase 3: Summer

**Step 1 — Place Clash Markers**
- The player **last** on the Order Track places Clash Markers I, II, III on the three Regions in any order they choose.
- Regions are resolved I → II → III.

**Step 2 — Resolve Clashes** *(repeat for each Region)*

For each Region in order:

**A. Reveal Cards** *(simultaneously)*
- All players reveal their face-down Faction Cards in that Region. These cards are now **Active**.

**B. Day Action Step** *(in turn order)*
- Each player activates any Day (sun icon) Actions and Commands on their Active cards.
- Any face-down cards added during this Step are revealed when all players complete their Action Step.
- Newly revealed cards' Day Actions/Commands may then be activated *in turn order*.

**C. Night Effects Step** *(simultaneously)*
- All Night (moon icon) Effects trigger automatically and are mandatory.
- Effects on cards are triggered even if that card was Eliminated during this step (all Effects resolve simultaneously).

**D. Tally Strength & Declare Outcome** *(simultaneously)*
- Total Strength = (Strength of all Active cards in this Region that have not taken part in any prior Clash in this Region this Round) + (1 per Supporter in Region not used in a prior Clash in this Region) + additional Strength from abilities.
- Player with **highest** total Strength wins.
- A player with no Active cards still contributes Supporter Strength.

**Resolving Tied Clashes:**
- Tied players *in turn order* choose: **play a face-down Faction Card** or **Pass**.
- If any tied player plays a card → repeat Steps A–D as a new Clash among tied players only.
- If **all** tied players Pass → Region is a tie; no rewards; no further Clashes in this Region this Round.

**E. Claim Rewards** *(winner only)*
1. **Choose a Location** — pick one of the two Locations in that Region.
2. **Herald Reward** — if your Herald is on the chosen Location: gain 1 Influence, then steal 1 Influence from each opponent whose Herald is also on that Location.
3. **Location Reward** — gain any Influence listed on the Location, then resolve any additional text.

Once Location Rewards are resolved, no further Clashes take place in this Region this Round.

After all three Regions are resolved → proceed to Autumn.

---

### Phase 4: Autumn

**Autumn Action Step** *(in turn order)*

Each player may do the following in any order and combination:
- **Govern** once with one card from their Hand
- **Journey** once with one card from their Hand
- Activate any number of Autumn (flame icon) Actions and Commands

**Govern:**
- Move one Faction Card with one or more Vote (hammer) icons from your Hand into a Council of your choice.
- The card contributes its Votes to that Council and provides that Council's benefit.

**Journey:**
- Move one Faction Card with one or more Lore icons from your Hand to the Lost Pile.
- Gain Lore Tokens equal to the Lore icons on that card.
- Whenever Lore Tokens are added to your Supply, you may **immediately** spend any amount of Lore to acquire cards from your Site of Power.

---

### Phase 5: Winter

**Step 1 — Winter Effects Step** *(simultaneously)*
- All Winter (snowflake icon) Effects trigger automatically and are mandatory.

**Step 2 — Cleanup** *(simultaneously)*
- **Remove Pieces:** Return all Heralds to their Player Boards. Move all Supporters on the Map to the Lost Pile.
- **Remove Clash Markers:** Return Clash Markers to their slot on Kingdom Board.
- **Remove Active Cards:** Move all remaining Active cards without Influence on them to their Discard Piles. Active cards with Influence on them: remove 1 Influence from each card (card stays Active).

**Step 3 — Advance the Round Track**
- Advance Round Marker one slot. If it was already in the final slot → the game ends.

---

## Winning the Game

At the end of the final Round:
1. Each player tallies total Influence in their Supply.
2. Player with the most Influence wins.

**Tiebreakers (in order):**
1. Player holding the Kingdom's Favour disc.
2. Player highest on the Order Track.

## Councils

The Councils Tile holds three Councils. Cards are added via Governing. There is no limit to cards per Council.

### Council of Relics (coins suit)
When claiming your **Herald Reward**, you may remove any number of your cards and/or markers here. Gain 1 Influence per total Votes discarded this way (in addition to the normal Herald Reward). Cards go to Discard Pile; markers return to Supply.

### Council of Secrets (scroll suit)
**Spring Action:** Place Faction Markers from your Supply on any Locations, equal to the number of Votes you have here.
- If a Location has **4 or more** of your markers → remove all your markers from that Location and claim its Location Reward (**ignoring all Influence** on the reward). If multiple Locations have 4+ markers, resolve them one at a time.
- Faction Markers persist Round to Round.
- **The Council of Secrets does not allow a Herald Reward, even if your Herald is on the chosen Location.**

### Council of Oaths (sword suit)
**Autumn Action:** Move Supporters from the Map and/or Lost Pile to your Player Board equal to the number of Votes you have here. If you have the **most** Votes in this Council, move one additional Supporter. (Must have at least 1 Vote in this Council to be eligible for "most.")

**Moving Cards into a Council:**
- Claiming the Castle Location Reward (Govern with Active card or card from Hand)
- Taking the Govern Action in Autumn (from Hand)
- Certain abilities

**Removing Cards from a Council:**
- Claiming Castle Location Reward removes all other cards from that Council (including yours).
- Actions/abilities that remove cards.
- Removed cards go to owner's Discard Pile unless otherwise stated.

## Sites of Power

Each Faction has its own Site of Power Tile with 5 cards:
- **Advanced Faction Cards** (identified by Lore Cost icon)
- **HQ Cards** (identified by HQ icon + Lore Cost)

**Acquiring:** Spend Lore Tokens equal to the card's Lore Cost. Can spend at any time you gain Lore Tokens, and may purchase multiple cards simultaneously.
- Advanced Faction Card acquired → goes to Hand (if Hand full → top of Deck)
- HQ Card acquired → goes to Supply (not Hand; stays there permanently)

**Site of Power Empty:** If no cards remain at game end → convert remaining Lore 2-for-1 into Influence.

## The Kingdom's Favour Disc

- Starts on Harvest Field Location.
- Claimed when a player wins the Harvest Field Location Reward (regardless of who held it before).
- Grants the holder access to their Faction-specific Kingdom's Favour Action (up to 3 uses per acquisition, tracked by rotating the disc I→II→III).
- After the third use → disc returns to Harvest Field Location.
- Re-claiming the disc resets it to III.
- **Tiebreaker** at game end.

## Commands

Commands are **bolded** on cards. They indicate which Step they activate in and whether optional or mandatory.

| Command | Icon | Step | Optional? | Limit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Ambush** | Day sun | Day Action Step | Optional | Once per Clash per card; may be used multiple times by same card in a Round |
| **Retreat** | Day sun | Day Action Step | Optional | Once per Clash per card; may be used multiple times by same card in a Round |
| **Flank** | Day sun | Day Action Step | Optional | Once per Clash per card; may be used multiple times by same card in a Round |
| **Rally** | Autumn flame | Autumn Action Step | Optional | Once per listed Step per card |
| **Deploy (X)** | Autumn flame | Autumn Action Step | Optional | Once per listed Step per card |
| **Deadly** | Night moon | Night Effects Step | **Mandatory** | Once per Clash per card; may be triggered multiple times by same card in a Round |

### Command Details

**Ambush (Day):** If this card is Active in the current Clash and has not already used Ambush this Clash, add a face-down Faction Card from your Hand to the Clash. That card is revealed after all players complete their Day Action Step.

**Retreat (Day):** Move any number of your Active cards in this card's Region to your Hand, including this card. Optionally also move your Herald and any Supporters in this Region back to your Player Board.

**Flank (Day):** Move this card to a different **unresolved** Region. It is now Active there. If no unresolved Regions remain, this card may not Flank.

**Rally (Self) (Autumn):** Move this Active card to your Hand.
**Rally (Any X) (Autumn):** Move up to X Active cards to your Hand. One of these may be this card.

**Deploy (X) (Autumn):** If this card is in your Hand, place it face-up next to a Region (it is now Active there). Place X Influence on it from the Reserve. The Influence stays on the card until Winter Cleanup (card remains Active until then).

**Deadly (Night):** Eliminate all opponents' Active cards in the current Clash. Eliminated cards are removed from the Clash and moved to the Lost Pile. Multiple Deadly commands trigger simultaneously — all involved cards are considered to have caused the Elimination.

## Traits

Traits are passive and always in effect.

| Trait | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Resilient** | If Eliminated, move to your Discard Pile instead of the Lost Pile |
| **Invulnerable** | Immune to Elimination by Deadly or any other Elimination ability |
| **Pathfinder** | If used to Journey, move to Discard Pile instead of Lost Pile; still gain Lore normally |

## Key Clarifications

### Active Cards
A Faction Card is **Active** if and only if it is face-up and next to a Region. Cards in Hand, Deck, Discard Pile, Supply, Lost Pile, Councils, or Occupying Kingdom Cards are **not** Active.

### Information
- **Private:** Cards in Hand; your face-down Bidding card; your face-down cards next to Regions.
- **Hidden:** Face-down cards in any Deck. No player may look at hidden information unless an ability states otherwise.
- **Public:** Everything else (Discard Piles, Lost Pile, Councils, Kingdom Discard, etc.)

### Printed vs. Gained
- **Printed:** Information on the card itself. Can be targeted/affected by abilities.
- **Gained:** Abilities temporarily added to the card. Effects targeting "printed" info do not affect gained info.

### Burn/Burned
A Burned component is permanently removed from the game (back to the box). It cannot be targeted or interacted with.

### Attrition
- Triggered when forced to draw from an empty Deck.
- Resolve: shuffle Discard Pile into new Deck, then reduce Hand Size by 1 (min 3), then draw to new Hand Size.

### Hand Size Limits
- **Min:** 3 | **Max:** 8
- If a player ever has more cards than their Hand Size (e.g., after Attrition), immediately discard down to Hand Size.

### Claiming Herald Reward vs. Location Reward
- Herald Reward: gain 1 Influence, steal 1 Influence from each opponent whose Herald is on the **chosen Location**.
- Location Reward: printed on Location tile.
- The Council of Secrets only grants a Location Reward when triggered — it does **not** allow a Herald Reward.

### Strength (Clash)
Strength contributed = Active cards in Region (not used in a prior Clash in that Region this Round) + 1 per Supporter in Region (not used in a prior Clash) + ability bonuses.

### Turn Order
In turn order Steps: start with player highest on Order Track, proceed downward. Last player receives Active Player Marker back to first player when all done.

## Setup Summary

1. **A** — Place Kingdom Board; place Councils Tile above it.
2. **B** — Shuffle Kingdom Cards; populate Great Road (3 face-down spaces, draw 3 face-up + flip top of deck for 4th face-up).
3. **C** — Place all Influence and Lore Tokens in the Reserve.
4. **D** — Place Kingdom's Favour disc on Harvest Field.
5. **E** — Place Season Marker in Spring slot.
6. **F** — Place Round Marker in slot 1.
7. **G** — Stack Clash Markers (III bottom, I top) in indicated slot.
8. **H** — Players choose or randomly determine Factions; take Player Boards.
9. **I** — Take Herald + 5 Supporters; place on Player Board.
10. **J** — Place Hand Size Marker at starting Hand Size (♦ icon position).
11. **K** — Take 4 Tactic Tiles; place in Tactics slot (Unexhausted side up). Follow any SETUP instructions on Tactics.
12. **L** — Take Faction Markers.
13. **M** — Place Site of Power Tile; find and separate Advanced Faction Cards + HQ Cards from Faction Deck; place them face-up on Site of Power. (5 cards per Faction.)
14. **N** — Search Faction Deck for Heir card (♦ icon); set aside. Shuffle remaining cards; place face-down as Deck. Take Heir into Hand; draw until you have 6 cards.
15. **O** — Determine starting order by Absolute Primogeniture (oldest first, then descending age) or randomly. Place Order Markers on Order Track.
16. **P** — First player on Order Track takes Active Player Marker.

## Advanced Setup (Optional)

Recommended for experienced players. May be used with any game length.

After Step A of setup:
1. Each player is dealt 5 face-down Kingdom Cards (may look, may discuss, may not show).
2. Players decide whether to take a Mulligan (redraw Hand). If yes: return all non-♦ Hand cards to Deck top, shuffle, redraw to Hand Size. (Once only.)
3. Each player chooses one of their 5 Kingdom Cards and places it face-down on their Player Board. Place a card from Hand face-down on top of it.
4. Unchosen Kingdom Cards are shuffled back into Kingdom Deck (keeping current face-up card aside until shuffling is done).
5. During Step O (determine order), reveal all face-down Kingdom Cards and the face-down Faction Cards on them — they Occupy the Kingdom Card. Any bold SETUP text resolves in turn order.
6. Players draw up to Hand Size if needed.

## Glossary Quick-Reference

| Term | Meaning |

| Eliminated | Removed from Clash to Lost Pile |
